## Value based design

As an ever-defining systemic practice, Circularity is best learnt together. The topic is so bushy that it needs a concerto of many minds to discover its best practices. Therefore the crowdlearning.

Learners need to wrap their head around the niches defined by their expertises and come back to each other to "sharpen the saw". They will need debates, allnighter discussions & slick keynotes to do so.
